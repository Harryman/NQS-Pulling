39 in³. Alterted V-twin Rules

Home | General Rules

  1. 1000 lbs. max
  2. 39 in.³ max
  3. Engine shielding rules: 1/8" steel or 3/16" aluminum on external side of cylinder extending from head gasket to frame. Shield must be attached to frame at the bottom and to the flywheel shield at the top to make rigid.
  4. Engines: Must be a commercially available lawn and/or garden tractor engine. Only configuration accepted is V-Twin type.
  5. Cylinder Head: Must be stock head with no external welding. Epoxy Okay. No valve restrictions
  6. Carburetor: Limited to one (1) carburetor with one (1) venturi with one (1) throttle butterfly. Maximum venturi size will be 1.200". No air entering after the 1.200" restriction.
  7. Steel flywheel mandatory, along with the general flywheel shielding rules of 1/8" steel or 3/16" aluminum 360 degrees.
  8. Fuel: Methanol only as per the General rules or VP C12 (supplied by the NQS). Pullers running gas needs show up to the pull with an EMPTY fuel tank. Each puller running gas will be given 1 quart prior to competing. If running gas the Puller will be charged an additional $5.00 per entry to cover the cost of fuel and administrating it.
  9. Wheelbase 56 inches maximum.
  10. Overall length 96 inches maximum.
  11. Top placing tractors will be checked for, but not limited to: Fuel and Cubic Inch Limit. 50.500 in³. zero tolerance. Formula for cubic inch is Measured bore x measured bore x measured stroke x .785 = Cubic Inch All bores measured 90 degrees from thrust side.
